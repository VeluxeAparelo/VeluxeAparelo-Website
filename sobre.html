Who are we?

VeluxeAparelo was born with a clear mission: to create the ultimate structured gaming ecosystem.

We are not a service agency; we are a disciplined social entity dedicated to maintaining organized, secure, and thriving digital spaces across Roblox, Discord, and Minecraft. We bridge the gap between casual gaming and professional community governance.

Our Core Pillars

Community First

Our heart lies in our members. We foster safe and engaging environments where players can socialize, compete, and grow within our Discord and Game Servers.

Strategic Partnerships

We actively collaborate with other clans and organizations to create joint events. Unity is key to our mutual success and expansion.

Strict Organization

Structure is everything. Our internal departments operate under high standards of quality and discipline, ensuring a chaos-free experience.

Total Security

Protected by the VA Intelligence Agency. We rigorously investigate threats and enforce fair play to protect our users' integrity.
