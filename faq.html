Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our community, game servers, security, and memberships.

What is VeluxeAparelo?

▼

VeluxeAparelo is a **social gaming community**. We focus on creating fun and organized environments for our members to hang out and play. We manage our own exclusive sections, including Roblox Groups (Military & Studios) and our own Minecraft Server.

Do you manage other people's servers?

▼

No. VeluxeAparelo is not a service agency. We do not offer management, consultancy, or bot development services for external servers. We strictly focus on developing, maintaining, and securing our own internal community ecosystem.

How can I play in your servers?

▼

You can join our community freely! Check our Services Page to find the direct IP for our Minecraft Server, links to our Roblox Groups, and the invite to our Discord.

What is the VA Intelligence Agency?

▼

The VAIA is our internal security division responsible for keeping our community safe. They investigate threats, exploiting, and ban evasion within VeluxeAparelo's servers. They never investigate real-life personal data.

I was Blacklisted. Can I appeal?

▼

No. Being "Blacklisted" is a permanent expulsion from the VeluxeAparelo community reserved for severe violations (raiding, serious threats, etc.). Blacklisted users are banned from all our platforms and cannot return.

How can I add my Organization?

▼

The "Organizations" page lists internal divisions or close allies. Adding an organization requires a special Validation Code provided by the Board of Directors. It is not open for public advertisement.

How do I get a VIP Rank?

▼

We use Patreon for all memberships and donations. By supporting us, you get exclusive perks within our Discord and Game Servers. Visit patreon.com/VeluxeAparelo.

Do you offer support?

▼

Yes, but only for our own platforms. If you have an issue inside our Minecraft server, Roblox groups, or Discord, please open a ticket in our Discord Server.
